# Business Presentations & Pitch Deck Portfolio

## 📌 Project Overview

This repository contains a collection of PowerPoint presentations developed for business, marketing, and entrepreneurial purposes. The projects demonstrate skills in presentation design, storytelling, market research, strategic thinking, branding, and business communication.

Each presentation is designed to effectively communicate ideas, business models, marketing strategies, and campaign concepts to target audiences.

---

## 📂 Projects Included

### 1. EYIC HealMe Pitch Deck

A startup pitch deck developed to present the HealMe business concept, including:

* Problem Statement
* Proposed Solution
* Market Opportunity
* Business Model
* Competitive Analysis
* Revenue Strategy
* Growth Potential

### 2. TWT Advertising Campaign (Dummy Project)

A mock advertising campaign designed to demonstrate:

* Brand Positioning
* Target Audience Analysis
* Marketing Objectives
* Campaign Strategy
* Promotional Activities
* Expected Outcomes

### 3. McFit Advertising Campaign (Dummy Project)

A conceptual advertising proposal focusing on:

* Brand Awareness
* Customer Engagement
* Marketing Communication
* Creative Campaign Development
* Digital Marketing Strategy
